Training at HolzFormArt

Apprenticeship

Exacting standards. In the quality of our work and the quality of our training.

Our joinery HolzFormArt in Neukirchen-Vluyn realises international projects in the areas of shop fitting and interior fit-outs. We provide brands and companies with the finishing touch with our madetomeasure interiors. We also produce individual pieces of furniture made exactly to the specifications and desires of private customers, too. Every project is different and every day is fascinating.

That also applies to our training programme at HolzFormArt. In our modern business enterprise, we offer apprenticeships for joiners and vocational training in office management. This includes a friendly team, interesting projects, further training options and, most of all: work that is fun. This brochure provides you with all the details you need.

Vocational training
at HolzFormArt.

  • Certified Office Manager

    Also for three years, this vocational training course deals with all the areas of administration within the company. In keeping with the training curriculum, you pass through all our specialist departments such as accounting, project management and management control. Always with a mentor at your side.

  • Joiner

    As part of our team at HolzFormArt, you will carry out your three-year apprenticeship as a joiner working on genuine orders. Apart from working in the workshop, you will pass through the various departments such as production planning, design and interior design, and project management, receiving comprehensive training in keeping with the training curriculum. You will have customer contact and a designated mentor in our company.

Sustainable. Successful. Individual. These principles guide our training at HolzFormArt. Sustainable means using yet preserving resources. APPLICATION DETAILS

Your application for an apprenticeship or on-the-job vocational training at HolzFormArt.

Joiner

For your application you will need:

  • a higher school-leaving certificate, a GCSE/ high school diploma or a basic secondary school certificate
  • a result of at least “Satisfactory” in mathematics and German on the leaving certificate
  • motivation and willingness to learn at school and at work
  • an aptitude for manual craft

Application phase

Apply in the period from 1 September to 20 November in any given year for an apprenticeship in the following year. If we find your application of interest, we will invite you to a personal interview. This is followed by a written aptitude test on the various themes associated with your apprenticeship. The final stage is one week of practical work experience to help you as an applicant and us as a training organisation to find out if we suit each other.

Certified Office Manager

For your application you will need:

  • the equivalent of at least GCSE or a high school diploma
  • good marks in mathematics, German and English

Application phase

Once we have received your documentation, if we should find your application particularly interesting, we will invite you to a personal interview. This interview presents you with the opportunity to outline your motivation for choosing this path and to explain what your training and career aspirations are. Just a few days later you will hear from us with the outcome.

Your application documents

This is what we need from every applicant:

  • letter of application
  • your CV in tabular form and a photo
  • anticipated type of school-leaving certificate and expected date of completion
  • a copy of your most recent school report and employer references
